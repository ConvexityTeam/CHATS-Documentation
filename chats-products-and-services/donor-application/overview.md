#  Overview

## key Features of the Donor application

Users with guest access to the platform.

#### Private Donor:

路        View NGO that has given Donor access

路        View Vendors assigned to campaigns and the campaigns

路        View Beneficiaries and their details

路        View total campaigns (plus Cash for Work) created, and their Beneficiaries

路        Total campaign funds amount spent on campaigns and their beneficiaries with complaints and resolved

路        The total amount on wallet spent and balance of NGO, Vendors and Beneficiaries.

路        Total campaigns, beneficiaries complaints and resolved

路        View campaign progress report(%) of Cash for work

路        View campaign progress(% and number of Beneficiaries in a campaign that has received and spent their fund to Vendor)

路        Campaign location of beneficiaries

#### Public Donor:

路        View Total Number of Beneficiaries and Vendors assigned to Campaign  from Campaign link

路        Total campaign funds amount spent on campaigns, and the number of beneficiaries and complaints and resolved

路        Total amount on wallet spent and balance of NGO, Vendors, and Beneficiaries.

路        Total complaints and resolved from Beneficiaries

路        View campaign progress report(%) of Cash for work

路        View campaign progress(% and number of Beneficiaries in a campaign that has received and spent their fund to Vendor)

路        Campaign location of beneficiaries
